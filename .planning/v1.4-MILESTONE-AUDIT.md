## Cross-Phase Integration (9/10 wired)

All exports from Phase 22 are consumed by Phase 23:
- Campaign.influencer_sheet_tab/influencer_sheet_id used by SheetMonitor
- SheetsClient.get_all_influencers(worksheet_name, spreadsheet_key_override) used by check_campaign_sheet
- No orphaned exports, no missing connections

**One scoping note:** The sheet monitor filters campaigns by `influencer_sheet_tab or influencer_sheet_id`, meaning campaigns using the default master sheet are not polled. This is intentional — default-sheet campaigns have no per-campaign tab to monitor. The requirement text ("each active campaign's sheet tab") inherently implies campaigns with a configured tab.

## Context

Use this example when the campaign targets multiple platforms and the content format is compatible across them (e.g., IG Reels cross-posted to TikTok). Content syndication reduces production workload for the creator while maximizing campaign reach. This is an effective way to increase deliverable count without proportionally increasing cost or creator effort.

## Email

Hi {name},

We're putting together a multi-platform campaign and think your content would be a perfect fit across both Instagram and TikTok. Here's what we had in mind:

**Campaign Package:**
- 1 IG Reel (primary content)
- 2 IG Stories (supporting content)
- 1 TikTok Video (cross-post of the IG Reel)
- **Usage Rights:** {usage_rights_duration}
- **Total Rate:** {bundled_rate}

The TikTok component would be a cross-post of the IG Reel, so there's no additional production work on your end -- just reposting the same content to reach your TikTok audience as well. This way, we get the multi-platform reach we're looking for, and you get a streamlined production process with one core piece of content.

We've structured the rate to reflect that the TikTok deliverable is a cross-post rather than original content. The total package gives us great coverage across both platforms while keeping things simple for you.

Let me know your thoughts! We're happy to adjust the package if you'd prefer a different mix of deliverables.

Best,
{sender_name}

## Key Tactics

- **Syndication:** Positions the cross-post as a benefit to the creator (less work) rather than a cost-cutting measure -- "streamlined production process" frames it positively
- **Cross-posting:** Explicitly states that the TikTok component is a cross-post, setting clear expectations about workload and why the bundled rate reflects that
- **Multi-platform:** Emphasizes the reach benefit of appearing on both platforms, which appeals to creators who care about growing their audience across channels
- **Bundling:** Wraps everything into a single package rate rather than pricing per-platform, which simplifies the conversation and focuses on total value

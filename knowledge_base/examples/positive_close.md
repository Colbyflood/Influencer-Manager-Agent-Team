## Context

Use this example when both parties have agreed on rate, deliverables, and usage rights. The goal is to confirm the deal clearly, recap all terms, and move into execution with specific next steps. This email should leave zero ambiguity about what was agreed.

## Email

Hi {name},

We're thrilled to confirm this partnership! We're genuinely excited about the content you'll be creating and can't wait to see it come together.

Here's a quick recap of what we've agreed on:

- **Deliverables:** {deliverables}
- **Rate:** {rate}
- **Usage Rights:** {usage_rights_duration}
- **Timeline:** Content to be posted by {deadline}

Next steps from our side:
1. We'll send over the SOW for your review and signature within the next 24 hours
2. Once signed, we'll share the content brief and any brand guidelines
3. Payment of {rate} will be processed within {payment_terms} of content going live

If you have any questions in the meantime, don't hesitate to reach out. Looking forward to working together!

Best,
{sender_name}

## Key Tactics

- **Confirmation:** Clearly recaps every agreed term (deliverables, rate, usage rights, timeline) so both parties are aligned and there's a written record
- **Next steps:** Provides a specific, numbered action plan so the creator knows exactly what happens next and when
- **Payment terms:** Proactively addresses payment timeline, which builds trust and professionalism
- **Warm tone:** Celebrates the deal without being over-the-top -- maintains the partnership energy established during negotiation
